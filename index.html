This.App.businessplan

  • Create a system where venues/clients and musicians can communicate and establish professional relationships
  • Facilitate contact and communicationi between site members

This.App.businessplan.musicians

  • “follow/friend” functionality—send friends notifications of user’s activity
  • Checkbox on profile — "I am looking for musicians to play together" (have them select genre and specialty)
  • Suggest users to follow/contact-match location | musical genre | specialty
  • Schedule of events on musicians' profiles
  • History of profiles visited—links to pages
  • History of videos watched
  • Pair up amateurs with teachers
  • Like or dislike — maybe have a zapscore — work on benefits for most liked users of the week
  • Endorsement section — musicians are endorsed by clients/venues
  • Give users ability to delete/approve reviews
  • Musicians can rate venues
    • Visible for musicians and other venues

This.App.businessplan.venues

  • Sign up for Venues
  • Email venues the "hot" musicians in their area (possibly charge for this service)
  • Venues can rate musicians
    • Visible to musicians and other venues
  • Display number of musicians hired by each venue
